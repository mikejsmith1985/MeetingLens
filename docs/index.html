MeetingLens

Capture a meeting's slides and shared screens, then get a screen-reader-friendly summary from Copilot. Everything you need to do is spoken aloud, and the right button is always selected for you — just press Enter when prompted.

What happens, step by step

  1. The Start button is already selected. Press Enter.
  2. Your browser asks what to share. Choose Entire Screen, then Share.
  3. Go to your meeting. MeetingLens takes screenshots on its own and says each one aloud. You don't need to do anything.
  4. When the meeting ends, come back to this tab. The Stop button is now selected — press Enter.
  5. Your screenshots download, the Copilot prompt is copied, and Copilot opens. Paste the prompt, attach the screenshots, and read the summary.
