MeetingLens

Capture a meeting's slides and shared screens, then get a screen-reader-friendly summary from Copilot. Everything you need to do is spoken aloud, and the right button is always selected for you — just press Enter when prompted.

What happens, step by step

  1. The Start button is already selected. Press Enter.
  2. Your browser opens a share box (read aloud by your screen reader). Press Tab until you hear Entire Screen, select it, then Tab to Share and press Enter.
  3. Go to your meeting. MeetingLens takes screenshots on its own and says each one aloud.
  4. When the meeting ends, come back to this tab. The Stop button is selected — press Enter.
  5. An Open Copilot button is then selected — press Enter to open Copilot, paste the prompt, and attach the single MeetingCaptures.pdf from your Downloads folder.

Your prompt is copied to the clipboard. In Copilot, paste it with Ctrl+V and attach the single file MeetingCaptures.pdf from your Downloads folder. If pasting doesn't work, copy it from the box below (Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C).

Save Copilot's answer as notes

Tab once to reach the Save button, then press Enter to save the summary as a file.

Setup — for the person configuring this

Set your organisation's Copilot web address and the screen-change sensitivity, then click Save a copy to send. That downloads a MeetingLens file with your settings built in — send that file to the person who will use it. Your settings stay on your computer and in the file you send; nothing is uploaded anywhere.
