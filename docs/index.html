MeetingLens

Capture a meeting's slides and shared screens, then get a screen-reader-friendly summary from Copilot. Everything is spoken aloud. Works entirely in your browser — nothing to install.

Ready. Press Start capture to begin.

When this tab is focused you can also press S start, X stop, R status.

How to use it

  1. Press Start capture. Your browser asks what to share — choose Entire Screen, then Share.
  2. Go to your meeting as normal. MeetingLens keeps taking screenshots on its own and says each one aloud.
  3. When the meeting ends, come back to this tab and press Stop & make summary.
  4. Your screenshots download to your Downloads folder, the prompt is copied to your clipboard, and Copilot opens in a new tab. Paste the prompt, attach the screenshots, and read Copilot's summary.
